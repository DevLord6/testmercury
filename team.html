Message by Yousuf Asif, CEO

“The best way to predict your future is to create it”.

Our entrepreneurial philosophy has crossed many ways, sometimes we have won, sometimes time was really hard to compete, but we have always stood up and had a great pleasure of doing every bit of it in high spirits. With this great pride and privilege I would like to address my Mercury family that during the whole circle of journey we have come a long way. The journey which begun in 1965, and when I see today the company has achieved a long term hard work success from being a local normal startup company to expand the wings in whole world to achieve new heights of success while earning the faith and trust of our all respectable clients and partners. Our leading pace has always been accredited to our culture, with a deep understanding that in order to improve the lives of our Dealer we must reflect our internal working culture and our promise for a wonderful world. Here at Mercury, the image which we have created in the market reflects our core universal values, proximity, and competitiveness. For us our clients are our asset and they are always first, identifying their needs and wants is the driving force that pulls us towards our eagerness to deliver. We have a clear vision that with time we have to grow more in our industry and have to become a market leader and for which we have to focus more on our core values which are lead, grow, deliver, sustain and protect. With my personal commitment I would like to assure that we will deliver what we promise and do is safely and sustainably. Hand in hand we take the journey to pioneer the way in this fast paced environment as we pave the way towards creating value for all our stakeholders and truly honor our vision towards a wonderful world. At the end I would like to say that I am very much optimistic about the future of the company and the roles that innovation and entrepreneurship will play to realize dreams. Our mission at Mercury is to support the future and I’m passionate about empowering our customers, our employees, our partners, our stakeholders to realize their dreams by working with us.

“Success is not final; failure is not fatal: it is the courage to continue that counts.”

Yousuf Asif
